About Me

Mapping Stories, Shaping Futures

Riska Aprilia Kuswati

Geospatial Researcher & Consultant

Jakarta, Indonesia

Riska Aprilia Kuswati is a geospatial researcher dedicated to addressing global environmental challenges through the lens of spatial science. With a passion for translating complex data into actionable insights, she bridges the gap between technology and sustainable development.

Education & Background

Holding an MSc in Geospatial Sciences (Hydrographic Surveying) with distinction from University College London, Riska brings over 5 years of experience as a geospatial consultant. She has contributed to numerous mining and construction projects across Indonesia, developing expertise in spatial analysis, remote sensing, and environmental assessment.

Current Research

Her current research addresses global environmental challenges, with a focus on mining impact assessment and mitigation for energy transition, while incorporating AI and machine learning to enhance geospatial analysis.

She is actively working to publish her research in high-impact journals and collaborate with international institutions on climate resilience and sustainable development projects.
