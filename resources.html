Jakarta Subsidence Climate Adaptation

Analysis of North Jakarta's land subsidence crisis with evidence-based interventions and adaptation strategies. Includes data analysis, visualization code, and simulation tools to model subsidence rates and climate change impacts on coastal vulnerability.

Papua Natural Capital

Revealing the hidden environmental cost of mining operations (case study: Grasberg Mine, Indonesia). Cutting-edge analysis of ecosystem destruction, carbon loss, and economic trade-offs. Quantifying the true impact of resource extraction on critical natural landscapes.
